On-Ice xGF — Design Directions

COL @ MIN · 2025-05-13 · Final/OT · Real data from D1

A — Centered Split
% lives in a fixed column. Bar diverges left/right from 50% midline. Value never moves far from center.
B — Heat Table
No bars. Color-coded % cell tells the whole story. Warm = dominant, gray = neutral, faded = dominated.
C — Dot Track
% printed inside a dot that travels to the exact position. Dot color = team. 50% rule is always visible.
D — B Split (Home | Away)
Heat table split into two columns — home left, away right. Half the rows per side. Team avg xGF% shown in the header. No eye travel.
E — Situation Split  ★ NEW
Single list sorted by 5v5 xGF%. Two grouped column sets: 5V5 (cols 2-3) and ALL SIT (cols 4-5). Team color on heat cell identifies side. No layout changes at width.
